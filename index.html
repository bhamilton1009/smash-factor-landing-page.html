From assessment to
peak performance.

Every phase is built from your screen results. This is the framework — your program will be personalized to your specific findings.

Week 1–2
Foundation
Screen, Assess & Baseline
Full TPI 16-point screen, clubhead speed baseline, grip strength and rotational power testing. Your corrective priority list is built. Program assigned: Hybrid or Elite track.
Weeks 2–6
Corrective
Corrective Phase — Close the Physical Gaps
Screen-specific Level 1 and Level 2 correctives from your TPI Mobility Database. Hip IR restoration, T-spine mobility, glute activation. 3D Strap proprioceptive drills. Foundation strength work begins Week 3.
Week 4
Re-Screen
First Re-Screen — What Has Improved?
Re-run key screens. What's passed gets loaded — correctives that worked get upgraded to strength movements. What's still limited gets a new corrective approach. Nothing static.
Weeks 5–10
Development
Strength & Rotational Power Development
TPI 3×3 strength matrix — hinge, squat, push, pull, anti-rotation, rotation. Chop Handle cable patterns. KB swings, med ball throws, power progressions. Your improved mobility gets loaded.
Weeks 9–12
Speed
Speed Development & Peak Output
SuperSpeed overspeed protocol, PAP complexes, peak-load lifts. Med ball power re-test. Final CHS measurement vs. Week 1 baseline. Your swing faults are noticeably reduced — your numbers prove it.
Week 12
Assessment
Final Re-Screen — Your Before & After
Every screen re-run. CHS re-tested. Scores compared to Week 1 baseline. You see the exact physical improvements and the distance gains — in measurable, objective data.
